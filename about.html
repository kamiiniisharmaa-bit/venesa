Our origin story

Founded in 2022, Vanesa started with a simple belief: online shopping should be fast, transparent, and human. Our founders noticed that most marketplaces prioritized volume over user experience — leading to delayed deliveries and complicated returns.

We built Vanesa from scratch, combining AI-powered logistics with handpicked premium brands. Today, we partner with over 2,000+ sellers and have fulfilment centers in three continents. Every package is treated like a gift, because we know your time matters.

From electronics to daily essentials, we are the new standard for instant commerce. And we are just getting started.
